What we are Doing
This year's 'Meet and Greet' will take place at the African American International Museum Foundation located at 1098 Firestone Ave., Memphis TN 38107. Reunion packets and t-shirts will be handed out at this time. We will also provide refreshments for everyone to enjoy while we catch up with each other. We will host this event from 6:00pm until 9:00pm


About the Museum
The African American Museum sponsors the Buffalo Soldier Museum, the Cotton Jubilee Museum, the African American Heritage Museum, the Black Memphian Museum, the Black Invention Museum, the Umoja Nature Center and the International Museum of Museum. The foundation is the owner of a 100-acre farm which has a educational farm and petting zoo that teaches children and their families regarding farm life, ecological system, green space, solar energy and basic family values.
